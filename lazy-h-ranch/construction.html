CONSTRUCTION

Still no cabin built, but the greenhouse was dried in just as the snow started to fly last year. The travel trailer is still out here and is my home-away-from-home. I also built a quick 7’x7’ outhouse that encloses a portable toilet and a composting toilet, one for short legs and one for longer legs. I mainly threw the building around it because one of your cousins/nephews wasn’t thrilled about a tarp surrounding the facilities on only three sides. Lately, I’ve been excavating for an underground storage shed. The excavation for the foundation is on hold while I finish a 150’ drainage ditch that’s up to 3’ deep so that if we do have a water incident, the water has somewhere to go. Digging that ditch with a pick and shovel has been arduous! (Did I mention there’s lots to do out here?) That shed will get the water pressure tank, 600 gallons of water storage, solar controllers, batteries and inverters. It’ll be good to have all that in so when people come out we’ll have running water and I don’t have to run the generators to keep charging the 500 devices they bring with them! Speaking of water, I had the well water tested again this year and it’s still as yummy as ever, maybe a little hard, but I don’t even think it’s as hard as my Tooele Water. So blessed that it ended up being half the depth (and cost) we were expecting and the water is usable! That’s about it for what’s new, not a lot of progress, but as my dad used to tell me, “I may be old, but I’m slow!” (I’m not sure… Did I mention, there’s lots to do out here? I’d love to have you visit so I can put you to work. I mean, show you around the place!) Here's a slideshow of photos of some of the construction.
